# Increment

It is a concrete stepping stone towards the product goal. It is additive to all prior increments and thoroughly verified so everything works together. To provide value, the increment must be **usable**.

Work is not part of the increment unless it meets the [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/definition_of_done|definition of done]].
