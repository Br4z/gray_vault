# Sprint review

It is a meeting where the scrum team and stakeholders collaborate to review what was done during the sprint and see how the product was improved (new features/functionality).

## Input

- The [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/artifacts/increment|increment]]) (what is done and usable) to inspect and discuss.

- Current [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/artifacts/product_backlog|product backlog]] (where priorities stand right now).

- Stakeholders feedback and questions.

## Output

**Shared understanding of the current increment** (what value was delivered) and updated product backlog.

## Typical format

1. Show what was completed (often a demo of the increment.

2. Discuss what was learned during the sprint.

3. Collect feedback and questions from stakeholders.

4. Agree on what is most valuable next (update priorities in the product backlog).
