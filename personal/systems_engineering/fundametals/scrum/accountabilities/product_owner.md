# Product owner

They are accountable for **maximizing the value of the product** and is the **single person responsible for managing the [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/artifacts/product_backlog|product backlog]]**

## Responsibilities

- Clearly expressing product backlog items.

- Ordering items to best achieve goals.

- Ensuring the backlog is visible, transparent and clear.

- Ensuring developers understand items enough to work on them.

The PO decides **priorities and value** (the **what**); developers decide the **how** to deliver work.

## How the PO shows up in the Scrum events

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_planning|Sprint planning]]: explain the objective and discuss the top product backlog items with the team.

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/daily|Daily]]: optional attendance; it should not be turned it into a status meeting.

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_review|Srint review]]: collaborate with stakeholders and the team, and gather feedback.

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_retrospective|Sprint retrospective]]: participates as part of the Scrum team.
