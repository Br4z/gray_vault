# Developers

They are accountable for a **usable [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/artifacts/increment|increment]]** each [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint|sprint]].

## Responsibilities

- Commit to the sprint goal.

- Forecast a selected number of features for the sprint.

- Present sprint achievements (often via demo in the [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_review|sprint_review]]).

- Help the [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/accountabilities/product_owner|product owner]] with [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/backlog_refinement|backlog refinement]].

## How the developers shows up in the Scrum events

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_planning|Sprint planning]]: forecast what will be developed during the sprint.

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/daily|Daily]]: review the progress against the plan, understand what is done/remaining and adjust the plan to meet the sprint goal.

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_review|Sprint review]]: help show what was done (and collaborate with stakeholders in reviewing it).

- [[personal/systems_engineering/fundametals/scrum/events/sprint_retrospective|Sprint retrospective]]: participates as part of the Scrum team.
