# Benefits

TDD brings several practical advantages across quality, speed and maintainability.

It improves test coverage, which helps teams catch bugs and errors quickly and get faster feedback while developing. because issues are detected earlier, it can reduce the time required for rework.

Over time, the discipline of writing tests first encourages cleaner and better designs and the resulting safety net gives confidence to change large architecture more easily.

As a result, teams tend to end up with code that is more flexible and easier to maintain.
